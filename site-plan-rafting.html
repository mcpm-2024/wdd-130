Overview

Purpose

The purpose of establishing an online presence for my rafting business is to share valuable information and knowledge about rafting with our audience. Through our website, we aim to provide the tailed insight into the different tours and destinations available, offer safety tips for water activities, recomend essential equipment and physical preparation, and educate visitors about the importance of environmental conservation. By sharing this information, with strive to attract an audience interested in rafting and establish ourselves as a trusted source of expertise in the field.

Audience

Who are the target customers?

My potential customers could be those seeking exiting and adventures experience, who are interested in participating in outdoor activities and water sports. The audience could range from young adults to families and groups of friends looking to have a hight quality experience and are willing to travel to participate in our excursions.

What does the audience expect to find accessing the website?

What the audience expects to find when accesing my website is detailed information about the different rafting tours and destinations offered, some pricing and booking options, safety tips and recommendations to enjoy rafting safely, some inspiring image showcasing the excitement of rafting adventures, information about the provided equipment, dress requirements, and what to expect on a typical rafting day.

How will my audience usually access my site?

My audience will primarily access my website through online searches, using devices such as cell phones, laptops, or desktop.
